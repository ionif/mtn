About

Mountain Bubble Tea is a startup founded in Los Alamos by Alex Ionkov, Cameron Art, and Phillip Martin. We are trying to bring quality, affordable bubble tea to Los Alamos and Santa Fe areas.

Idea

  • Bubble Tea (Boba) is a flavored milk tea with tapioca pearls.
  • Los Alamos and Santa Fe do not currently have Bubble Tea businesses.
  • Many bubble tea businesses charge high prices for drinks.
  • Many peers have expressed interest in this beverage.

    • Outreach

  • Currently we are in the process of obtaining both a business permit as well as food selling permits.
  • We are in talks with the school to sell at the cafeteria and/or sports events.
